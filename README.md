## 📌 What is FuelX?

FuelX is a mobile-first fuel tracking web app built for bikes, scooties, and cars. Most riders track fuel on WhatsApp, notes apps, or just don't track at all — which means zero visibility into where fuel money actually goes every month.

FuelX fixes that. Log a refill in seconds, get your mileage auto-calculated, and see your weekly and monthly fuel spend in a clean dashboard — all from your phone.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Quick Refill Logging** — Log fuel amount, company, and odometer in seconds
- **Auto Mileage Calculation** — Every refill automatically computes your km/l
- **Weekly & Monthly Analytics** — Visual spend and fuel usage breakdown
- **Fuel Company Defaults** — Smart liter estimates for Shell, BPCL, HPCL, and more
- **Refill History** — Full log with filters by date and company
- **Vehicle Profile** — Store your vehicle details and baseline mileage
- **Location Auto-fetch** — Nearest petrol bunk/area detected automatically
- **Secure Authentication** — Email/password login with encrypted sessions
- **Mobile-First UI** — Built and optimized for on-the-go use
